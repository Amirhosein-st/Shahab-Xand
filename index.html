Hello, I’m Xand!

My name is Shahab Zandieh (Xand) and I am a product designer. I have 6 years of experience in designing digital products. I started my career in 2001 as a graphic designer and later ventured into motion design due to my personal interest. In 2015, I became familiar with the concepts of UI and UX, enhancing my knowledge in product design. Currently, I work as the head of product design.

Having experience in graphic and motion design has greatly aided me in becoming a product designer and understanding the issues better. Throughout my professional journey, I have been able to form several product design teams and assist companies in defining their product strategies and creating digital products.
